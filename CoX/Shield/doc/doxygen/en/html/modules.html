The PDMA has nine channels of DMA (Peripheral-to-Memory , Memory-to-Peripheral or Memory-to-Memory). For each PDMA channel (PDMA CH0~CH8), there is one word buffer as transfer buffer between the Peripherals APB devices and Memory
